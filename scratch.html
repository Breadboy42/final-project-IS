Masterpieces of Storytelling

There are good shows, and then there are shows that change television forever. Here are my two favorite shows.

  1. Breaking Bad

    A high school chemistry teacher turned meth kingpin. The character development of walter white is unmatched.

    • Protagonist: Walter White
    • Best Episode: "Ozymandias"
    • Rating: 10/10

  2. Attack on Titan

    It's a shame most people won't watch this show simply because it's animated. The plot twists and storytelling are leagues above anything else I've ever seen.

    • Protagonist: Eren Yeager
    • Best Episode: Memories of the Future
    • Rating: 10/10
